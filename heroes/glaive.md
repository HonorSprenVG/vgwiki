# Glaive

Brutal axe warrior who can knock enemies out of position.

#### Heroic Perk: Hunt The Weak

Glaive's critical strikes cleave in a 4-meter cone in front of him.

* Cleave deals half damage to minions.

* Health: 834 - 2503
* Energy: 275 - 440
* Armor: 20 - 60
* Shield: 20 - 60
* Weapon: 80 - 153
* Atk Speed: 100% - 113.2%
* Range: 2.6
* Move Speed: 3.3
