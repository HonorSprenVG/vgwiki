# Catherine

Disruptive tank with lots of stuns & a powerful silence.

#### Heroic Perk: Captain of the Guard

Catherine gains 1 armor & shield every time she stuns or silences an enemy hero with her abilities.

###### Stats Lvl 1-12

* Health: 800 - 2673
* Energy: 200 - 464
* Armor: 20 - 60
* Shield: 20 - 60
* Weapon: 74 - 141
* Atk Speed: 100% - 136.3%
* Range: 1.5
* Move Speed: 3.4
