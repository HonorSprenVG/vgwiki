# Ozo

Acrobatic monkey with immense self-healing.

#### Heroic Perk: Carnie Luck

Ozo receives 15%-30% additional healing, barrier & fortified health from all sources.

* This does not affect health regeneration.

###### Stats Lvl 1-12

* Health: 769 - 2536
* Energy: 350 - 650
* Armor: 20 - 60
* Shield: 20 - 60
* Weapon: 80 - 157
* Atk Speed: 100% - 136.3%
* Range: 1.7
* Move Speed: 3.3
